How to Play

  • Reach 21 without going over. Dealer stands on soft 17.
  • Blackjack pays 3 to 2. All other wins pay 1 to 1.
  • Split once when cards match in value. Split aces get one card each.
  • Double on any two cards to take exactly one more card.
  • Insurance is offered when the dealer shows an Ace.
