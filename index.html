How to play
Beat the dealer by getting closer to 21 without going over. Blackjack (A + 10-value) pays 3:2.
- Hit: take a card
- Stand: hold and let dealer play
- Double: double bet, take one card, then stand
- Split: if you have an exact pair, split into two hands (Aces get one card each)
- Insurance: offered vs dealer Ace only
This is single-player casino-style blackjack with a multi-deck shoe and dealer H17/S17 setting.
