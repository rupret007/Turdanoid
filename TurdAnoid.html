TurdAnoid Turbo

Break every destructible brick, keep your lives, and chain silly power-ups for chaos runs.

  • Move Paddle: mouse, touch, or A/D + arrow keys
  • Launch Ball: Space or tap/click
  • Pause: P or Space while live
  • Restart: R
  • Goal: survive escalating chaos and clear level 69

Tip: combo streaks and stink buildup make scoring and effects way more intense.
