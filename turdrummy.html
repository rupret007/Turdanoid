How TurdRummy Works

Goal: first to 100 by making melds and minimizing deadwood.

  • Set = 3-4 of the same rank.
  • Run = 3+ consecutive cards in one suit.
  • Knock when your post-discard deadwood is 10 or less.
  • Gin when post-discard deadwood is exactly 0.
  • Undercut gives bonus points to the defender.

On mobile, draw/discard/knock/gin are grouped in one button dock.
