TurdSpades

Classic 4-player Spades: you + North partner vs West/East bots.

  • Bid tricks before play starts.
  • You must follow lead suit when possible.
  • Spades are trump and cannot lead until broken, unless your hand is all spades.
  • Make bid: +10 per bid trick, plus +1 per overtrick bag.
  • Every 10 bags = -100 penalty.

Controls: choose bid with +/- then Lock Bid. During play, select card then Play Selected. Press Enter to confirm bid/play.
